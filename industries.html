Industry Partners

Evergreen Valley Foods
Ready-to-Eat Meals & Frozen Entrées

Project: Packaging Line Automation & Controls Modernization

Tradewinds partnered with Evergreen Valley Foods to modernize an aging packaging line supporting their growing ready-to-eat product portfolio. The project included PLC upgrades, standardized HMI screens, and improved changeover logic to support frequent SKU changes.

Impact:
  • Increased line throughput by over 20%
  • Reduced operator errors through intuitive HMI design
  • Improved reliability and reduced unplanned downtime during peak production
Redwood Creamery Co.
Cultured Dairy & Specialty Yogurt Products

Project: Process Automation & CIP Improvements

Tradewinds supported Redwood Creamery with automation improvements to batch processing and CIP systems. Enhancements focused on recipe consistency, automated sequencing, and improved operator visibility into critical process parameters.

Impact:
  • Improved batch repeatability and quality consistency
  • Reduced CIP cycle times and overall utility usage
  • Strengthened food safety and sanitation compliance
Harvest Field Bakery

Impact:
